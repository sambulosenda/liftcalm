# LiftCalm Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 15 June 2026**

LiftCalm is built privacy-first. Your training data belongs to you and stays on your device. This policy explains what that means in plain terms.

## The short version

LiftCalm does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no third-party SDKs. We have no servers and never see your data.

## What data the app stores

All data you create in LiftCalm — workouts, sets, exercises, routines, body-weight and settings — is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's on-device database (SwiftData). It never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to enable iCloud Sync (below).

We do not have access to this data. It is not sent to us or to anyone else.

## iCloud Sync (optional)

If you enable iCloud Sync, your training data is synchronised across your own Apple devices through your **private iCloud account** using Apple's CloudKit. This data is stored in your personal iCloud, governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/), and is not accessible to us. iCloud Sync is off by default; you can turn it off at any time in Settings.

## Purchases

LiftCalm Plus is a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase processed entirely by **Apple**. We never receive or store your payment details. Purchase and restore are handled by Apple's StoreKit; see [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Notifications

If you grant permission, LiftCalm schedules **local notifications** (rest-timer alerts and an optional daily reminder) directly on your device. These are not sent through any server and contain no personal data leaving the device. You can disable them in Settings or in the iOS Settings app.

## Health data

LiftCalm does not currently read from or write to Apple Health. If a future version offers an optional Health integration, it will request your explicit permission first and this policy will be updated.

## Data export and deletion

You can export all your data at any time (CSV/JSON) from within the app. To delete your data, delete the relevant records in the app or remove the app from your device; deleting the app removes its local data. If iCloud Sync was enabled, you can also remove the app's data from iCloud in the iOS Settings app under your Apple Account.

## Children's privacy

LiftCalm does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Material changes will be reflected in an app update.
