Privacy Policy

LiftCalm · Last updated 17 June 2026

LiftCalm is built to be private by default. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with your information.

The short version

LiftCalm does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything you log stays on your device. We do not operate any servers that receive your data, we have no accounts, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

What we store, and where

  • Your training data (workouts, exercises, routines, settings) is stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device database. It never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.
  • App preferences (such as your goal, rest timer, and whether LiftCalm Plus is unlocked) are stored in your device’s standard local settings.

What we do not collect

  • No name, email address, or account — LiftCalm has no sign-up.
  • No analytics, usage tracking, or behavioural profiling.
  • No advertising identifiers and no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.
  • No location, contacts, photos, or health data.

Notifications

If you enable rest-timer or reminder notifications, they are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No notification content is sent to us or any third party.

Purchases

LiftCalm Plus is a one-time in-app purchase processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment details. Whether you have unlocked Plus is determined on-device from your App Store purchase history; that status is not reported to us.

Exporting and deleting your data

You can export your full training history at any time from Settings → Your Data. Because all data is stored on your device, deleting the app removes all of it. We hold no copy and therefore cannot access, restore, or delete data on your behalf.

Children

LiftCalm does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. As no data is collected, no special handling is required.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. The current version always governs your use of the app.
